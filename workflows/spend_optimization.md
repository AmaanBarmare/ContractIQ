# Workflow: Spend Optimization

Analyzes the entire contract portfolio to surface where money is being wasted, where overlaps exist, and where renegotiation should happen.

---

## When It Triggers

- User selects "Run Spend Analysis" from the dashboard
- Scheduled monthly run (if enabled)
- CFO or VP requests portfolio-level savings analysis

---

## What It Analyzes

### Tool Overlap Detection
Identifies vendors in the same functional category:
- Two project management tools (e.g. Asana + Monday.com)
- Two internal wiki tools (e.g. Confluence + Notion)
- Two video conferencing tools (e.g. Zoom + Teams)

Calculates combined annual spend in overlapping categories and flags for consolidation.

### Above-Market Pricing
For each vendor, compares the extracted unit rate against Tavily-sourced pricing benchmarks. Flags vendors where the current rate is more than 10% above the market midpoint.

### Underutilized Commitments
Flags contracts where:
- Minimum seat commitment appears higher than estimated actual usage
- Annual contract value is high but usage signals suggest low adoption

### Inactive or Low-Value Vendors
Identifies vendors with:
- Contract value under $5,000/year (may not justify contract management overhead)
- No renewal owner assigned
- Original contract owner has left the company

### Renewal Pipeline Exposure
Calculates total spend renewing in each quarter and flags quarters with unusually high exposure.

# Workflow: Executive Reporting

Produces board-ready portfolio summaries for CFO, VP, or board-level consumption on demand.

---

## When It Triggers

- CFO or VP selects "Generate Executive Summary" from the dashboard
- Board meeting preparation (manual trigger)
- Monthly/quarterly scheduled summary (if enabled)

---

## Output Components

### Spend Summary
- Total annual contract spend by category
- Top 10 vendors by annual spend
- Spend trend vs. prior period (if prior data exists)
- Budget exposure by quarter (upcoming renewals)

### Risk Summary
- Number of contracts with Critical / High / Medium risk flags
- Top 3 risks requiring executive attention
- Contracts with imminent deadlines

### Savings Pipeline
- Identified savings opportunities with estimated values
- Progress on previously identified opportunities
- Total savings realized vs. opportunities identified

### Portfolio Health
- % of contracts with complete structured data
- % of contracts with assigned renewal owners
- % of vendors with current compliance documentation (SOC 2, DPA)
- Contracts missing critical documents

## Formatting

Executive summaries are generated as:
- In-app view (default)
- Downloadable PDF (one click)
- Email-ready text (paste into email or Slack)

All figures are grounded in the actual extracted contract data — no manual aggregation required.
